Author: Amittai Axelrod

Most of the letters in the comic have been replaced with "X", except for a handful which have been replaced by a number from 1 to 33. Take the numbered letters and place them in order to get RED JUICY COCK DISH RHYMES WITH COCO VAN. The answer is COQ AU VIN.
