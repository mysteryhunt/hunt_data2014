Solution to Find Your Way Across

Authors: Alina Griner, Hanna Starobinets

In this word search are hidden the names of MIT classes, and the words MATRICULATION and GRADUATION. All the words are connected, and make up a maze, where the goal is to get from MATRICULATION to GRADUATION traversing the class names. Looking at the turning points along the solution path, the letters spell "THE ANSWER IS DOWN PAT".
