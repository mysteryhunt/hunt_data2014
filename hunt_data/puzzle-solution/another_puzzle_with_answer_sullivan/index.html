Solution to Another Puzzle with the Answer SULLIVAN

Author: Anand Sarwate

This is a White Queen Puzzle, so the solvers have the "answer word" from the meta already. For this puzzle the meta-answer is SULLIVAN.

There are three parts to this puzzle: a menu, tasting notes, and checks.

This puzzle relies on associated words to dollar values via the simple mapping a = $0.01, b = $0.02, etc.

At the top of the puzzle the solvers are given a menu with a list of words and prices. Reading down the first letter of the menu items spells MIKADO COMPOSER SURNAME, which is a clue for SULLIVAN.

Each word in the menu is associated with two prices, a "sing." (single) and a "doz." (dozen) price. The single price is the dollar value of the word. The dozen price is equal to 12 * ( VALUE(WORD) - DISCOUNT ), where each discount is in the range $0.01 to $0.26. The discounts spell down, in clue order, FIND OYSTER AT EACH PRICE.

The next part of the puzzle is a set of "tasting notes," which are in the form of the wordplay part of cryptic clues. The definition for each clues is the name of an oyster, and the clues are given in alphabetical order by oyster name. There are many oyster varieties. All the ones chosen here were taken from The Oyster Guide by Rowan Jacobsen. Here are the solutions to the clues:

  • Plan trades right for nothing
    • BLUEPRINT with R->O = BLUEPOINT
  • Cobra's dormant indoors
    • coBRASDORmant = BRASDOR
  • Cap inside PhD author
    • CHAM gets HAT inside = CHATHAM
  • Reverse habit gets out... out

  • Cart has no right to lazy breaks

  • Gelding's internals allowed

  • The Spanish honker crashed without energy

  • A peanut flower is idiotic without bush

  • One good pony horrifically encased in rock
    • anagram(I + G + PONY) inside FLINT = FLYINGPOINT
  • Loud sphere concealed but unheated
    • F + ORB + HIDDEN - H = FORBIDDEN
  • A month to follow grand hesitation

  • "Kiss?" "Uh... awkward..."
