Solution to Please Remain Seated

Author: James Clark

Twenty city names are listed, followed by twenty image/video pairs. The image in each pair is of a (real) roller coaster, and the video is of a ride on a roller coaster designed in the game Roller Coaster Tycoon 3 (RCT3).

The first step is to identify all of the real roller coasters. Each one can be found in an amusement park in one of the cities (and each city is represented).

The RCT3 roller coasters don't match up with the real roller coasters, since their tracks are not similar to the real roller coasters, and the roller coaster types are in some cases wildly different. However, it can be noticed that some of the RCT3 roller coasters have unusual track layouts, with lots of over/under crossings.

In fact, all of the track layouts are knots (in the knot theory sense). None can be unravelled to form the "unknot", and all are in minimal form with respect to their crossing numbers.

In case solvers do not discover this, reading down the first letters of the real coaster names in the order given gives the clue 'CROSSING NUMBERS INDEX'.
