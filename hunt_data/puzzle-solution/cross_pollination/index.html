Solution to Cross-Pollination

Authors: Kevin G. Der and Quinn Mahoney

Complete the two crosswords. Each BEE 'cross-pollinates' between the grids—an entry will stop at BEE in one grid and continue from the BEE in the other grid.

Those entries are:

  • IVE BEEN HAD / FRISBEE GAME
  • ALBEES / AMBEER
  • HEART BEEPS / WATER BEETLE
  • SHEBEENS / DE BEERS
  • COLD BEERS / DONT BE EVIL
  • GO BEEP / US BEEF
  • SUGAR BEETS / BLUE BEECH
  • TUBE EYE / WILDEBEEST

Each crossword grid provides a clue when folded into the origami flower above it.
Fold the left grid into a poinsettia and read counterclockwise for WEDS TECH.
Fold the right grid into a rose and read in the center for FRI NYT.

Complete the crossword in The Tech from the Wednesday before the Hunt (January 15), and fold it into a sunflower, revealing the word NUMBER around the petals.
Complete the crossword in The New York Times from the Friday the Hunt started (January 17), and fold it into a cherry blossom, revealing the word THEORY around the center.
The answer to this puzzle is NUMBER THEORY.
